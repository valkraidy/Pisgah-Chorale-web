About Us

VISION

To establish a leading sustainable international non-denominational choir, celebrated for exceptional performers and performances.

MISSION

Inspiring a global audience, Pisgah Chorale seeks to nurture a harmonious world through the transformative essence of music, guiding people towards heavenly experiences.

PROJECT

Pisgah Chorale hits the streets with heart and melody, feeding the hungry and lifting spirits at the close of each year.

EVENT

Pisgah Chorale marked its sensational first anniversary on June 3rd at the vibrant Legon SDA Church. We've been the musical ministers at numerous churches and gatherings, spreading joy and harmony since our inception.
